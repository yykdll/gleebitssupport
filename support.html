‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create my first GleeBit?

Tap the "+" button in the top right corner of the main screen. Fill in your happy memory with a title, description, choose an importance level, select a category, and optionally add up to 9 photos. Then tap "Save" to create your GleeBit.

Q: What's the difference between "sealed" and "opened" GleeBits?

Sealed GleeBits appear as closed envelopes - you can see the title but not the content. Opening them reveals your memory. You can "seal" them again to rediscover them later. This creates a delightful surprise when you revisit old memories!

Q: How many GleeBits can I create for free?

Free users can create up to 3 categories with 20 GleeBits each (60 total). Premium users get unlimited categories and GleeBits. You can upgrade to premium with a one-time purchase.

Q: Are my photos and memories private?

Absolutely! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or have access to your personal content. Your memories stay completely private to you.

Q: Can I backup my GleeBits?

Your GleeBits are automatically included in your device's iCloud backup (if enabled). Premium users can also export their data. We recommend keeping regular device backups to protect your memories.

Q: How do I restore my premium purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to make the original purchase.

Q: Can I edit or delete GleeBits after creating them?

Yes! Tap any GleeBit to view its details, then use the "Edit" button to modify it or "Delete" to remove it. You can also long-press a GleeBit card for quick actions.
